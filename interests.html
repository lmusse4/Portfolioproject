To Travel is to Live

My Interest

Travel, food, and shopping - three of my absolute favorite things. There's nothing quite like the thrill of exploring a new place, trying out local cuisine, and finding unique treasures to bring back home. As someone who loves to travel, I've had the opportunity to sample a wide variety of foods from all over and every destination has its own unique flavors and specialties. I love stepping out of my comfort zone and trying dishes I've never heard of before, and I always make a point to ask locals for their recommendations. Of course, no trip would be complete without a little bit of shopping. Whether I'm browsing through a bustling market or perusing high-end boutiques, I always keep an eye out for items that are both beautiful and meaningful. I love picking up locally made crafts and artwork to bring back home as souvenirs, and I'm always on the lookout for new pieces to add to my wardrobe. For me, the best part of travel is the way it broadens my horizons and exposes me to new experiences. Whether I'm savoring a new flavor or discovering a hidden gem of a shop, every trip has the potential to open up a whole new world of possibilities. Hopefully my next trip will look like this!

Leyla Musse
